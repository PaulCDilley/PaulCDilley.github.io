This project is digitizing the trilingual (Bohairic Coptic–Latin–Arabic) glossaries found in Athanasius Kircher's *Lingua Aegyptiaca Restituta* (Rome, 1643), via the digitized copy held by the Bibliothèque nationale de France (Gallica).

The extraction code used to produce the transcription is available at [github.com/PaulCDilley/kircher-bohairic-glossaries](https://github.com/PaulCDilley/kircher-bohairic-glossaries).

The transcribed glossary data (original and revised) will be posted here once the Coptic readings have been checked.
